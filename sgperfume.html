Project overview

SG Perfume, a growing online fragrance retailer, faced operational setbacks due to an outdated Shopify 1.0 setup and a rising number of Return to Origin (RTO) cases. The existing site lacked modern checkout flows, responsive design, and user-friendly navigation. Our goal was to upgrade the platform to Shopify 2.0 and create a seamless, conversion-driven customer experience.

We delivered a high-performance Shopify 2.0 store tailored to SG Perfume’s brand identity. With fast-loading pages, mobile-first design, optimized checkout, and improved product discovery, the new platform enhances shopping convenience and reduces friction across devices.

Project challenge, solution & result

SG Perfume's Shopify 1.0 store was not equipped to handle modern eCommerce demands. A high rate of RTOs was affecting logistics and customer satisfaction. The site’s poor responsiveness, outdated layout, and inefficient checkout contributed to cart abandonment and lost sales opportunities.

We migrated the store to Shopify 2.0, deploying a fully custom theme designed for high performance, faster checkout, and superior mobile usability. Key functionalities were rebuilt with a focus on reducing RTOs through better address validation and smoother customer journeys.

The result is a sleek, responsive storefront that improved customer retention, accelerated checkout speed, and significantly minimized RTOs. SG Perfume now benefits from a flexible platform to support long-term growth and customer satisfaction.

SG Perfume faced several issues with their legacy Shopify setup:

High RTO Rates

Incomplete addresses and poor order validation led to a spike in returned shipments, increasing logistics costs.

Outdated Shopify Version

The store was still running on Shopify 1.0, lacking access to new features, performance upgrades, and customization options.

Poor Checkout Experience

The multi-step checkout process was slow and not optimized for conversions, especially on mobile devices.

Non-Responsive Design

The website design wasn’t mobile-first, leading to usability issues and low engagement from mobile users.

We delivered a comprehensive solution to modernize SG Perfume's eCommerce operations:

Migration to Shopify 2.0

Upgraded the site to Shopify 2.0 with zero downtime, ensuring access to better performance, flexibility, and app compatibility.

Custom Theme Development

Built a mobile-optimized, high-converting theme that reflects SG Perfume’s branding and improves usability.

Enhanced Checkout Flow

Introduced one-click checkout enhancements, address verification, and optimized payment gateway options.

Analytics & Automation

Integrated apps for abandoned cart recovery, delivery tracking, and RTO prevention to streamline order fulfillment.

The reimagined SG Perfume store brought measurable gains across the board:

Lower RTO Rates

Improved checkout validation and delivery workflows reduced return-to-origin cases by over 40%.

Faster Checkout

Streamlined checkout experience increased transaction completion rates, especially on mobile.

Mobile-First Performance

The new responsive design led to longer session durations and lower bounce rates.

Scalable Shopify 2.0 Architecture

SG Perfume is now equipped with a future-ready storefront that supports marketing, automation, and personalization with ease.
