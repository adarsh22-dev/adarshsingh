  • date: 1 September, 2024

Project overview

Pur Shilajit, a premium natural health supplement brand, needed a high-performance Shopify website to support their direct-to-consumer launch. With no existing site or branding online, our challenge was to build the entire store from the ground up with a focus on speed, usability, and conversion optimization.

We designed and developed a custom Shopify theme using Tailwind CSS, delivering a lightning-fast, mobile-first experience. The result was a clean, minimal design that reflected the brand’s purity, built to convert visitors into customers while offering a flexible backend for content and product management.

Project challenge, solution & result

With a tight two-week timeline, no prior Shopify setup, and a highly visual product, the challenge was to quickly create a fully functional and visually compelling online store that emphasized trust and simplicity.

We developed a custom Shopify theme entirely in Tailwind CSS, integrating optimized product sections, educational content blocks, and a fast checkout experience. Every component was built with performance and scalability in mind.

The final site went live within two weeks, delivering an intuitive shopping experience, increased brand trust, and a strong foundation for ongoing SEO and digital growth.

Key challenges we addressed while launching the Pur Shilajit Shopify store included:

No Pre-existing Infrastructure

The brand had no online presence, requiring full setup of Shopify store, theme, products, and content.

Tight Two-Week Timeline

The site needed to be fully developed, tested, and launched within 14 days from concept to completion.

Emphasis on Speed and UX

Fast load times and mobile-optimized layouts were critical for D2C performance and conversion.

Custom UI Design

The interface had to reflect purity, trust, and wellness through bespoke visuals and layout.

To bring the brand to life digitally, we implemented the following solutions:

Custom Shopify Theme

Developed a lightweight, high-performing Shopify theme using Tailwind CSS for full control and consistency.

Mobile-First Design

Every section was crafted for seamless mobile performance to support on-the-go shoppers.

Conversion-Focused Layouts

Included trust-building content, sticky add-to-cart, and customer education areas to increase sales.

Rapid Development & Launch

Used agile sprints to design, build, test, and deploy the site within two weeks.

The final Shopify store for Pur Shilajit delivered measurable success:

Launched in 14 Days

From concept to live store in just two weeks with a fully custom codebase and CMS-friendly setup.

Fast Load Speeds

Lean Tailwind CSS architecture and asset optimization resulted in excellent Core Web Vitals scores.

Improved User Engagement

Clean design and UX increased time on site and encouraged repeat visits and inquiries.

Easy Backend Management

The Shopify backend was customized to allow the brand team to manage content without developer support.
